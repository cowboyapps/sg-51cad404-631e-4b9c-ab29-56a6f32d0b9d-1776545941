# IPTV Business Platform

## Vision
A complete multi-tenant SaaS platform enabling IPTV service providers to launch professional websites with integrated customer management and automated billing systems. Three user tiers: Platform admins manage IPTV businesses, business owners customize their sites and manage customers, end customers subscribe and manage their accounts.

## Design
**Color System:**
- `--primary: 222 84% 11%` (deep navy #0F172A) — authority, trust
- `--accent: 187 94% 43%` (bright cyan #06B6D4) — CTAs, highlights
- `--background: 40 56% 97%` (warm cream #FAF8F5) — soften tech feel
- `--foreground: 222 47% 15%` (dark slate) — readable text
- `--muted: 220 13% 91%` (light grey) — cards, secondary surfaces
- `--card: 0 0% 100%` (white) — elevated content

**Typography:**
- Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans (600, 700) — modern SaaS authority
- Body: Work Sans (400, 500) — readable in dense tables

**Style:** Clean card-based layouts, subtle shadows for depth, generous spacing in marketing pages, tighter density in admin dashboards. Status badges color-coded, metric cards prominent, data tables with hover states.

## Features
- **Master Platform:** Marketing site, business owner sign-up, master admin dashboard managing all IPTV businesses
- **Business Dashboard:** Website builder, branding config, customer management, billing automation, subscription plans
- **Customer Portal:** Account sign-up, subscription management, billing history, service details, payment methods
- **Billing System:** Automated invoicing, payment processing, subscription plans, usage tracking, dunning management
